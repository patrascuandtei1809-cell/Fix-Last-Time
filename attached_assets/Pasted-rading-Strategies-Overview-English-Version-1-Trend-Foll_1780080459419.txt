rading Strategies Overview (English Version)
1. Trend Following
Idea:
Buy when the market is going up, sell/short when it’s going down.

Indicators:

EMA

SMA

MACD

ADX

Example:
EMA 50 crosses above EMA 200 → BUY
→ called “Golden Cross”

Works best: in trending markets
Fails: in sideways/choppy markets

2. Scalping
Idea:
Many fast trades with small profits.

Targets:
0.1% – 1% per trade

Timeframes:
1m, 5m

Uses:

Order flow

Volume spikes

VWAP

Liquidity zones

Requires:

Fast execution

Low fees

High discipline

3. Day Trading
Open and close positions within the same day.

Uses:

Support/resistance

RSI

Volume

Patterns

Advantage:
No overnight risk

4. Swing Trading
Hold trades for days or weeks.

Focus:

Bigger moves

Indicators:

Fibonacci

EMA

MACD

Market structure

More relaxed than scalping.

5. Mean Reversion
Idea:
Price returns to the average.

Buy when:

price is “too low”

Sell when:

price is “too high”

Indicators:

Bollinger Bands

RSI

Z-score

6. Breakout Trading
Enter when price breaks:

resistance

support

consolidation

Look for:

High volume

Strong momentum

Risk:
Fake breakouts

7. Arbitrage
Profit from price differences between exchanges.

Types:

Spatial arbitrage

Triangular arbitrage

Funding arbitrage

Requires:

Speed

Capital

Good API

8. Market Making
Place:

buy orders

sell orders

Profit = spread

Risk: volatility spikes

Used by:

HFT firms

9. Grid Trading
Bot places orders at fixed intervals.

Example:
Buy every $100 drop

Works: in sideways markets
Fails: in strong trends

10. Momentum Trading
Buy assets that are already moving strongly.

Uses:

Volume

Breakouts

Relative strength

Common in crypto/meme coins.

11. News Trading
Trade based on:

News

Listings

CPI

FED

ETF approvals

Very volatile.

12. Funding Rate Strategy
Used in futures.

Exploit:

Positive/negative funding

Popular on:

Binance Futures

Bybit

13. Statistical Arbitrage
Math-based strategies:

Pair trading

Cointegration

Regression

Used by quantitative funds.

14. Pair Trading
Example:

ETH & BTC move similarly

If one deviates → trade the difference

15. DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging)
Buy fixed amounts regularly.

Example:

$100 BTC weekly

Reduces volatility impact.

16. AI / Machine Learning Trading
Uses:

LSTM

XGBoost

Reinforcement Learning

Data:

Price

Volume

Sentiment

Order book

Reality: hard to make consistent profit.

17. Order Flow Trading
Analyze:

Liquidity

Bids/asks

Footprint

Heatmaps

Tools:

Bookmap

TensorCharts

18. Smart Money Concepts (SMC)
Popular online.

Concepts:

Liquidity grabs

Order blocks

Fair value gaps

BOS / CHOCH

19. Price Action
Minimal indicators.

Focus on:

Candles

Support/resistance

Market structure
