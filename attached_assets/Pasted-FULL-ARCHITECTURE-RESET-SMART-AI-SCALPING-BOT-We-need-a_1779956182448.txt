==================================================
MARKET REGIME SYSTEM
==================================================

Create market_regime.py.

The bot must classify market state:

- DEAD
- RANGE
- TREND
- VOLATILE

Rules:

DEAD:
- very low ATR
- tiny candles
- low volume
→ no trade

RANGE:
- sideways market
- mixed candles
→ only allow very high score trades

TREND:
- EMA structure clear
- momentum continuing
→ allow normal aggressive scalping

VOLATILE:
- large candles
- volume spike
→ allow trades but reduce size if risk is high

The bot must NOT treat every market the same.

==================================================
SCORING SYSTEM
==================================================

scorer.py must create a 0–100 score per symbol.

Score factors:

1. EMA structure
- EMA9 above EMA21 for BUY
- EMA9 below EMA21 for SELL
- slope strength

2. Momentum
- current momentum
- momentum acceleration
- continuation strength

3. Candle quality
- strong body
- low opposite wick
- continuation candles

4. Volume
- current volume vs average
- volume expansion

5. Volatility
- ATR rising
- enough movement to scalp

6. Market regime bonus/penalty
- TREND = bonus
- RANGE = penalty
- DEAD = reject

7. Fake move penalty
- big wick
- sudden reversal candle
- weak volume breakout

Output:

{
  "symbol": "BTCUSDT",
  "direction": "BUY/SELL/HOLD",
  "score": 0-100,
  "probability": 0-100,
  "regime": "TREND/RANGE/DEAD/VOLATILE",
  "reason": "short explanation"
}
