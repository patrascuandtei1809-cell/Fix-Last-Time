## What the full leaderboard shows

- **Timeframe is the single biggest factor.** Every 5m, 15m, and 1h cell across
  all strategies and symbols is **negative** after fees. The gross edge at these
  frames is smaller than the ~0.24% round-trip cost.
- **4h is the only frame that clears fees**, and only for higher-timeframe
  momentum/trend strategies (V2, Trend Pullback, Donchian) on **ETH and SOL**.
- **Best honest candidate: `EMA_MACD_RSI_VOLUME_V2` @ 4h.**
  - SOL +1.019%/trade (PF 1.59, Sharpe 1.24)
  - ETH +0.993%/trade (PF 1.72, Sharpe 1.65)
  - BTC −0.052%/trade (PF 0.95) — essentially break-even, slightly negative.

## Honest caveats (why this is NOT yet a green light to trade live)

1. **Not robust across symbols.** No strategy×timeframe is positive on all
   three coins. V2 @ 4h is strong on ETH+SOL but break-even/negative on BTC.
2. **Single window, modest sample.** 4h/360d gives only ~27–48 trades per
   symbol — too few to split into meaningful walk-forward folds, and a single
   360d window can be flattered by one favorable regime.
3. **Long-only.** These HTF strategies only take longs; performance leans on the
   window's net upward drift.

## Recommendation

The live auto-disable gate remains **default-safe (allowlist empty → no
auto-trading)**. The clear signal is *where* to focus next: **4h higher-timeframe
momentum on ETH/SOL**, not 1m/5m/15m scalping. Before risking live money, this
candidate needs out-of-sample confirmation across additional windows (longer
history or a separate hold-out period). That confirmation is best run off the
sandbox (no 120s cap) — see the proposed follow-up.
